Introduction

User Experience in Documentation Portals

For companies, it has become convenient to publish technical documentation for several products together with other product related information in one documentation portal to serve several user groups (Antidot 2020; Ziegler and Beier 2014). Documentation portals carry the company’s brand. The experience users have in such a portal directly connects back to the brand (Lee, Lee, and Choi 2018).
